About Us

Get-Mate is a travel community for cultural exchange, adventure weekends, working holidays and exploring India. We provide the most recent travel tips, news, advice to plan your own journey and to find a perfect host for you. We help you find a travel buddy who is just a click away.

Who We Are?

We are a team of travel and adventure enthusiasts looking for travel partners to be our hosts to provide lots of opportunities to new travel mates, to find exactly the right travel companion whom they are looking for to share the journey with. As Get Mate community, we want to unlock various travel experiences to find a travel buddy from around the world with common plans and interests.
